# Data types

- **S-expressions**
	- **Atoms**
		- Independent values
		- Examples: `a`, `B`, `#t`, `#f`
	- **Pairs**
		- Grouping of two atoms.
		- Structure: `(s1 . s2)`
	
- **Lists**
	- Grouping of atoms.
	- The empty list `()` is an atom.
	- Structure: `(s1 . L)` where L is another list.
